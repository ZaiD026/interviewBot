ABOUT US

Interview Bot

Interview Bot is a replacement for Interviewer for technical and non technical interviews. Product is made to help out companies better manage human resources, take interviews quicker and help hiring managers find suitable professionals for their companies. Interview Bot uses State of Art AI models for speech processing. It comes with inbuilt anti-cheat measures like eye tracking, Identity checker. Interview bot will also be able to judge the confidence of the candidate during the interview process.

First


The interviewer makes the interview for the candidate by making questions and adding the answers and selects the question to be asked in the interview and the candiate username and password is created and sent to them in mail.

Second

After recieving the credentials the candidate logins in his account and then he has to fill all the details documents and has to capture image which will be verified using AI model with his documents

Third

After filling and verification of documents now the candidate jumps up to interwiew page where the candidate has to deliever the interview. The candidate will have to open the webcam and microphone for the interview there his/her video and audio will be recorded and sent the interviewer.
The candidate will be given 2 minutes for each question during which his motions will be monitored using AI model.

Fourth

After the completion of interview, the score for the candiate is given on the basis of his confidence accuracy of the answer and the results are send to the interviewer where he gets al the detials of the candidate even his audio and video of the interview
The candidate can give only one interview

Last

After reviewing the interview by the interviewer the result of the interview is send to the candiate by the email in the form of PDF with the links if the video of the answers given by the respective candidate
